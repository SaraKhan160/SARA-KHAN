TRINITY INSTITUTE OF PROFESSIONAL STUDIES


ABOUT US

Trinity Institute of Professional Studies(TIPS), Dwarka is a premier educational institute located at a stone's throw distance from Sector 10 Metro Station, Dwarka (opposite pillar no. 1160).The Institute is NAAC accredited and has been ranked A+ Grade by SFRC, Government of Delhi. TIPS is one of the most well-known and top colleges, affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha(GGSIP) University.


THE LOGO

The Logo of the college represents its mission with the words "Light to enlighten". Enlighten comes from the metaphor that ignorance is a state of being "in the dark, "and that knowledge is illuminating. We believe that education has the power to provide deep insights which can lead to individual transformation.
